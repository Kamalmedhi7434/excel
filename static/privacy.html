Privacy Policy

Hi there! I care about your privacy, and I want you to feel confident using my Excel Dashboard app.

  • 🗂️ How your files are handled: When you upload an Excel or CSV file, it’s processed on the server side only to generate visualizations.
  • 💾 No storage, ever: I do not save or store any uploaded files on the server. The data is used in real time to create visuals—and that's it.
  • 🔁 Temporary, secure processing: Your file is handled only during your session. Once the visuals are generated, it’s automatically discarded.
  • 🧩 Third-party libraries: This app uses trusted open-source libraries like Flask, Pandas, and Plotly. These are used only during processing and do not store your data.
  • 🕵️ Zero tracking: I don’t track or analyze your file contents, and no personal information is collected.
